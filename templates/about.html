Crafting Chicago’s Finest Record Cabinets

I’m Mike Quinn, a Chicago-based furniture builder specializing in custom record cabinets, dining tables, and mid-century modern design. Every piece is crafted by hand with attention to detail and a deep love for the process.

MQ Woodworking started in my garage and has grown into a full-service studio focused on timeless pieces built to last. Whether you’re a vinyl collector or looking for a custom heirloom, I’d love to build something just for you.

From Vinyl Lover to Woodworker

I grew up flipping through crates at record stores and dreaming of a piece of furniture worthy of my collection. After studying design and learning traditional woodworking, I opened my Lakeview garage workshop. Today, MQ Woodworking joins my passion for vinyl with the craftsmanship I’ve honed—naming each piece after a Chicago street and handcrafting it from start to finish.
